The following is a clean, intuitive Flutter application that I designed to give real-time weather information to users of the app. It will be capable of providing the end-user with the current temperatures, humidity, and other detailed weather forecasts in the location of their choosing. It would have an intuitive interface and effortlessly integrate with strong, accurate weather APIs that give you that most current information in an instant. This project makes it fast and easy for you to check the weather during your day's planning and in preparation for your travel.
