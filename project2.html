Planto is an innovative app designed to assist users in predicting plant diseases. While it currently features only the front-end model, it showcases the potential of a comprehensive plant health management tool. Built using Flutter and Dart, Planto incorporates a user-friendly interface with a seamless sign-up and login process, followed by engaging introductory pages.
On the main screen, users can upload plant images either from the gallery or directly through the camera. Although the app doesn't yet have a backend, it provides a default result page to simulate disease prediction. The design and concept of Planto are entirely original, reflecting a personal touch in both idea and execution.
